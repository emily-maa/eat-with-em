Gochujang Pasta

Get ready to savor the goodness of our Spicy and Creamy Gochujang Pasta! Packed with flavor and a hint of heat, this dish is perfect for anyone craving a wholesome and tasty meal. Tender chicken, along with onion, spinach, and tomato, comes together to make a nutritious option for lunch or dinner. It's a deliciously simple way to satisfy your taste buds!

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried pasta
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp reserved pasta water
  • 2 tbsp gochujang paste
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 large onion, diced
  • 3 cups baby spinach
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes, diced
  • 2 chicken thighs
  • salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and chili powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Add chicken thigh to a bowl with 1 tbsp of oil, and salt, pepper, paprika, and chili powder to taste. Rub the spices onto both sides of the chicken.
  2. Air fry the chicken thighs at 400F for 8-12 minutes on each side, until golden add cooked through the center
  3. In a pot, boil water and add salt generously. Add pasta into the pot and cook until al dente according to package instructions
  4. Heat a pan over medium-low heat and add oil.
  5. Add minced garlic, onion, and sundried tomatoes to the pan and saute until the onion is translucent.
  6. Add the tomato and spinach and saute for another 2-3 minutes
  7. Allow the air-fried chicken to cool for 5-10 minutes, then cut it into strips.
  8. Combine the chicken and pasta into the pan and mix.
  9. Add gochujang, along with salt and pepper to taste. Mix to combine and top with shredded cheese.

Enjoy:)
