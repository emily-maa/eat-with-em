Soy Garlic Green Beans

The blend of sweetness, savory garlic, and the rich umami of soy sauce make these green beans the perfect choice for a healthy side dish. It perfectly complements a bowl of steaming rice and your protein of choice. Whether you're in a hurry or simply looking to add more greens to your meal, these green beans are a deliciously satisfying choice.

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 pound green beans, trimmed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium-low heat and add oil.
  2. Add minced garlic to the pan and saut√© briefly.
  3. Toss in the green beans.
  4. Add brown sugar and soy sauce, then mix.
  5. Cook for about 15 minutes on medium-low heat until beans are tender, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove from heat and serve.

Enjoy:)
